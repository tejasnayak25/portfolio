About

Hi, I'm Tejas Nayak, a passionate and self-taught fullstack developer with over 2 years of experience in building dynamic and user-focused web applications. My journey into the world of coding began with curiosity and quickly grew into a full-fledged passion for crafting innovative digital solutions.

Over the past two years, I've honed my skills through various self-driven projects, exploring different facets of software development. My toolkit includes a diverse set of technologies, including Flutter, C, C++, Javascript, Python, Next.js, React.js, Three.js, MySQL, Node.js, Firebase, Tailwind CSS, and more. From creating AI-powered tools to developing complex web applications, I've always been driven by a desire to build technology that empowers creativity.

While I haven't had formal company experience, my work speaks for itself. I've built and deployed a range of applications that showcase my ability to take an idea from concept to completion, solving real-world problems along the way. I believe that my diverse project experience has given me a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end development, and I'm constantly learning and adapting to new technologies.

Whether it's through my personal projects or contributions to open-source communities, I'm committed to using my skills to make a positive impact. I'm always on the lookout for new challenges and opportunities to grow as a developer.
